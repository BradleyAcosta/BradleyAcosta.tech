Project Objective:

 This project aims to assist students and receptionists in creating university accounts for new students at the Polytechnic University of Puerto Rico. It will also help students choose their majors and classes. The system will facilitate communication with professors and receptionists and provide answers to frequently asked questions, ensuring students feel confident in their decision to study at the university. Additionally, the system will offer detailed information about the university and its various majors.

Features:
This software is capable of making the students and admissions officers be able to communicate if they have questions, calendar/appointments, report any information, and manage to get information about the university.
The administrator controls members and manages changes of any type of news, appointments, and activity.
The admission officers can issue/return is maintained in the students database. The member’s details can be fetched by the professors from the database as and when required. The student members are also allowed to view their account information.
